My name is Sandra Szczepaniak and, privately, I am a proud mom of three wonderful children, while professionally – a passionate advocate of beauty and women’s strength. My journey in the beauty industry began in 2016, when my daughter was just a few months old. At that time, I was searching for an opportunity that would allow me to balance motherhood with personal growth and financial independence. That is how my business was born – something that over time became not only my profession, but also my true fulfillment.

Since 2019, I have been running my business and training courses in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, UK. I am a certified trainer for Luxury Eye, and my skills have been recognized multiple times in both online and live championships. The trophies I have earned proudly decorate my salon, serving as a reminder that passion and perseverance always lead to success.

I specialize in delivering accredited training courses, all rigorously verified for quality and content by Beauty Direct. My mission is not only to share technical knowledge, but also to inspire women to believe in themselves and their potential – so that every student leaves my training with confidence and readiness to achieve her dreams.

I still work daily with clients because I believe that, to be an effective trainer, you must live what you teach, stay up to date with industry trends, and always remain “in shape.” Thanks to this, I can share not only theory, but also practical, real-world experience straight from the stylist’s chair.

My motto? Beauty is confidence – and confidence opens the door to everything.
